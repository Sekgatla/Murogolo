Learning Phases

We offer three CAPS curriculum phases from Grade R through Grade 7

Grade R – 3
Foundation Phase
The critical early years where learners build core literacy and numeracy skills that form the bedrock of all future learning.
  • Home Language
  • Mathematics
  • Life Skills
Grade 4 – 6
Intermediate Phase
Learners expand their knowledge base across a broader range of subjects, developing critical thinking and subject-specific skills.
  • Languages
  • Mathematics
  • Natural Sciences & Technology
  • Social Sciences
Grade 7
Senior Phase
Our Grade 7 learners are prepared thoroughly for the transition to high school, with a full academic programme.
  • All intermediate subjects
  • Economic & Management Sciences
  • Creative Arts
