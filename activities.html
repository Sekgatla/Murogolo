Sports & Recreation at Murogolo Primary School

Sports & Recreation

Physical activity builds teamwork, discipline, and healthy habits for life

Soccer
Our most popular sport, with regular inter-school matches and a dedicated school team that participates in district competitions.
Athletics
Track and field events are held annually, with learners competing at school, cluster, and district level in events like running and long jump.
Netball
Netball is offered to our female learners as a team sport that develops coordination, communication, and leadership skills.
Culture, Arts & Learning at Murogolo Primary School

Culture & Events

Celebrating our heritage and building community spirit throughout the year

Cultural Day
An annual celebration of our Venda heritage, featuring traditional dress, music, dance, and food that connects learners to their roots.
Spelling & Maths Bees
Academic competitions that encourage learners to engage enthusiastically with core subjects and showcase their knowledge.
Choir & Performances
Our school choir performs at regional festivals and community events, nurturing musical talent and confidence in our learners.
Parents' Day
A termly event bringing together parents, guardians, and educators to celebrate learner achievements and discuss progress.
Year-End Concert
A festive end-of-year celebration where learners showcase their talents in drama, dance, and music to the whole community.
Community Outreach
We participate in local clean-up initiatives and community support projects, teaching learners the value of giving back.

Help Support Our Activities

Sports kits, musical instruments, and event materials depend on community support. Every donation helps our learners thrive.
