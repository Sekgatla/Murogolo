Support Our Learners

Your generosity directly improves the lives of children in Ha Muila Village, Limpopo.

Why Your Support Matters

Murogolo Primary School is a No-Fee government school, meaning we rely on community generosity to provide learners with resources beyond the basics.

Many of our learners come from households with limited income. Every rand donated goes directly toward improving the quality of education and life at our school.

Books & Stationery

Help us keep every learner equipped with the learning materials they need.

Sports Equipment

Donate soccer balls, netball kits, and athletics gear for our school teams.

School Uniforms

Help ensure every child attends school with dignity and a sense of belonging.

Facility Improvements

Contribute to classroom maintenance, gardens, and general school infrastructure.
