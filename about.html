About Us

Discover the history, mission, and dedicated team behind Murogolo Primary School in Ha Muila.

Our Background

Rooted in the heart of Ha Muila Village, Limpopo

Murogolo Primary School has been a cornerstone of education for the Ha Muila community for decades. We started with a simple vision: to ensure every child has access to quality basic education near their home.

As a government-funded No-Fee school, we proudly serve learners from Grade R through Grade 7, following the official CAPS curriculum under the Limpopo Department of Education.

Our school is governed by an active School Governing Body (SGB) that works closely with parents, educators, and local community leaders to continuously improve our facilities and learning environment.
