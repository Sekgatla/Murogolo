Admissions

Join the Murogolo family. We welcome learners from our community.

Application Process

Follow these simple steps to enroll your child

01

Visit the School Office

Come to the administration office on any school day between 07:30 and 14:00.

02

Collect an Application Form

Pick up the enrollment form and complete it with your child's details.

03

Submit Required Documents

Attach all the required documents listed below and return the completed form.

04

Await Confirmation

The school will review your application and contact you with placement confirmation.

Required Documents

Please bring originals and copies of the following

Child's Birth Certificate

Unabridged birth certificate from the Department of Home Affairs.

Parent or Guardian ID

South African ID document or valid passport of the parent/guardian.

Proof of Residence

A utility bill or affidavit confirming your address in the Ha Muila area.

Previous School Report

Latest progress report (where applicable — not required for Grade R).

Need help? Contact our administration office at 082 668 8165 during school hours.

No-Fee Public School

Murogolo is a No-Fee School

As a government-funded No-Fee school, we do not charge school fees. Every child in our community has the right to quality education, regardless of financial circumstance.
